Overview

What is Loom AI?

Loom is the leading async video messaging platform used by 25 million+ people at 400,000+ companies. AI-powered features automatically summarize recordings, generate titles, remove filler words, and create action items from your videos.

Loom has transformed how teams communicate asynchronously. Instead of scheduling yet another meeting, record a quick video message and share it instantly. The recipient can watch it on their own schedule and respond with their own Loom.

Loom AI takes this to the next level. Every recording is automatically summarized, key points are extracted, and action items are identified — so your team can get the gist of a video without watching the entire thing.

Used by remote and hybrid teams at companies like HubSpot, Atlassian, and Airbnb, Loom AI reduces unnecessary meetings and improves team communication efficiency dramatically.
